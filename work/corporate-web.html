THE BRIEF
As AAI grows, we need a new website to improve our online presence of the company. It is the face of the company for the users of the WWW. Many of them make their first impression of the company based on their first glance at the website.

I took the responsibility to redesign and develop the website utilizing Webflow (a platform for web development). The goal was to rebranding the corporate website and increase customer engagement.
value proposition
Informational: The website's content provides overview of the company's products and technologies, as well as use cases that they enable.
Conversion: The website contains contact information simplifying the first interaction of potential business partners with the company.
‍HR: exciting and intriguing description of the company's business area may attract professionals to join the team.
‍Branding: The website should convey the company's branding identity. We define our company image as "Premium Quality" "Innovative" and "High-Tech".
Stylying
To enhance the feeling of "Premium Quality", we decided to utilize to add a dark blue into our theme colors. The dark blue serves as a balanced complement to the original green color.

Previously we used "Futura"and "Roboto"as our primary fonts, but the feeling was not "Innovative" enough. After some research, we selected a more modern and clean font named "Basier" as our new company font.

FEEDBACK AND ITERATION
In the design process, it's essential to collect feedback and iterate accordingly. Having the overall layout ready, we began to use a sharing board to collect feedback from stakeholders. Stakeholders may have various kinds of opinions, but not all of them should be taken. It was strategic to deal with the feedback from stakeholders. After a few iterations, we have a finalized version that all stakeholders satisfy with.
