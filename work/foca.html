What is FOCA
Foca is an App created by me and a friend of mine.
BACKGROUND
Resulting from the Pandemic, both of us started to remote work since early 2020. But we found it hard to stay concentrated at home. Also we had some neck and back pain lacking of exercise.

That’s why we came up with the idea -- to create an App that helps people cultivate productive and healthy work habits.
