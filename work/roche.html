WHAT IS SMART ADHERE APP

The Roche Tech-Dev and Molecule teams seek opportunities to design and development of an application for IPF patients, in order to improve their adherence to the treatment. IPF is a chronic lung disease and the average life expectancy following diagnosis is about four years. Adherence to the treatment can play an important role to the patients' life quality and length. However current patients' adherence to the treatment is relatively low. That's why we decided to create the Smart Adhere App, to help increase patients' adherence to their treatments.

BEHAVIOR APPROACH
Patient persistent to Esbriet molecule has been shown to be influenced by the onset of side effects and the long term lack of perceived benefit. We collaborated with an agency focused on behaviour neuroscience to analyze the behavioural drivers behind patient persistence and assess the validity of digital tools as a solution for driving increased persistence.
USER RESEARCH
We analyzed the user research conducted by the molecule team as well as scientific literature related to IPF, patients and physicians as well as medication persistence to uncover key behaviour findings. We collected both quantitative and qualitative data. The data about topics discussed among patients at diagnosis n social medias and patient forums as the figure below shows. It demonstrates that patients are eager to know about more treatment options, to understand the disease and the prognosis of the disease. In the interviews, patients commented that they hoped that the diagnosis could be regarded earlier.

CONVERGENT:  USER JOURNEY
Journey 1: Working through the "Understanding IPF & Esbriet" course to build user's knowledge
Journey 2: Viewing and inputting information in "My Treatment" and "My Dose", and then being prompted to start a "Medication Journal" to reflect on the start of treatment.
Journey 3: Viewing a personalised daily feed in "My News" to make use of the app habit forming, and provide the user with the information they need to stay persistent.
