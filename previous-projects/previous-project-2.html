Transmetics App
What is transmetics
Transmetics is a startup project built from scratch. It's an App that helps foreigners finding suitable French cosmetics. French cosmetics are popular, however it is hard for people who are not French speakers to understand the descriptions on packages. It might lead to problems if users buy wrong skin care products that are not desired for them. I leaded the product design from scratch in a team of four.
